Hi! I'm Sullivan, an incoming PhD Student in Materials Science at Northwestern University

I am first year PhD student at the Northwestern Materials Science and Engineering department with a research focus in computational materials science.

My main research interests lie in computational materials science and engineering, including the applications of molecular dynamics simulations and machine learning to novel materials and in developing predictive models for broad ranges of material properties. I also have an interest in material-based experiment, including liquid crystals and quantum materials.
